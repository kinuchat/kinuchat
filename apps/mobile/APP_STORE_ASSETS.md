### Concept
Modern, minimal app icon for "Kinu" secure mesh messenger

### Design Elements
- **Primary element**: Stylized speech bubble or message icon
- **Secondary element**: Subtle mesh/network pattern, connected nodes, or signal waves
- **Style**: Flat/material design with rounded corners
- **Feeling**: Secure, connected, modern, trustworthy
